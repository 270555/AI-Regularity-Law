        <h2>🚨 What Problem Does This Solve?</h2>
        <p>
            Artificial intelligence is increasingly used in hiring, healthcare,

            unregulated, leading to serious risks.
        </p>
        <ul>
            <li>Bias and discrimination in AI decisions</li>
            <li>Lack of accountability when AI causes harm</li>
            <li>Privacy violations from excessive data collection</li>
            <li>Deployment of unsafe or untested AI systems</li>
        </ul>
    </div>

    <div class="card">
        <h2>📜 Main Rules of the Law</h2>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Mandatory Testing:</strong> All AI systems must pass safety and bias testing before public use.</li>
            <li><strong>Transparency:</strong> Companies must explain how their AI systems make decisions.</li>
            <li><strong>Accountability:</strong> Developers and companies are legally responsible for damages caused by AI.</li>
            <li><strong>Data Protection:</strong> AI systems must limit data collection and protect user privacy.</li>
            <li><strong>Government Oversight:</strong> A national agency will monitor high-risk AI systems.</li>
        </ul>
    </div>

    <div class="card">
        <h2>👥 Who Benefits?</h2>
        <ul>
            <li>Citizens protected from harmful or biased AI decisions</li>

            <li>Businesses that benefit from clear, fair regulations</li>
            <li>Developers who follow ethical standards</li>
        </ul>
    </div>
